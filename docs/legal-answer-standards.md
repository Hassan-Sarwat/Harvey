# Legal Answer Standards

Legal Q&A must be written for business and technical users, not legal specialists.

Every answer should include:

- Plain-language summary.
- Practical recommendation for the user's use case.
- Company playbook basis with rule id or citation.
- Legal basis from Legal Data Hub or fallback evidence.
- Clear escalation warning where legal judgment, illegality, ambiguity, or high-value deviation is involved.

Answers must not:

- Claim to replace legal counsel.
- Hide uncertainty.
- Cite fallback evidence as if it came from a live query.
- Approve illegal or blocker-severity deviations.
